ABOUT

Paddy Waggin' is a Irish Canadian folk rock band hailing from Vancouver BC. Rooted in traditional Irish folk with rock/punk influences. They play catchy original songs, Trad Irish and cover songs from newer Celtic groups. With smoke & strong whiskey vocals and crowd pleasing chants their sound is infectious so get ready to dance and sing along to your favorite tunes. Cheers to friends, music and Good times!
